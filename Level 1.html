Level 1: Beginner Level

Level 1 covers the basics of digital music with respect to Ableton Live. It goes in depth on explaining what digital music is, playing MIDI notes and recording audio, arranging clips to create and export audio pieces. These will create a foundational understanding of Digital Music for the student. Level 1 is built on the assumption that the student is learning digital music from the ground up. These lessons teach the student how to create using only their laptop, but they can use external equipment such as MIDI keyboards, audio interfaces and microphones to complete the lessons.

Lessons to expect:

1-01: What is Digital Music?
1-02: MIDI versus Audio, MIDI notes in Ableton
1-03: Playing MIDI drums in Ableton
1-04: Recording audio in Ableton
1-05: Session view versus arrangement view
1-06: Composition of a simple track
